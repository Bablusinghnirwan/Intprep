How to Use IntPrep

A quick, step-by-step guide to get you practicing like a pro.

1

Set up your API Key (First Step)

Click the Gear icon ⚙️ in the header and paste your personal Gemini API Key. Get one for free at Google AI Studio . Your key is saved locally in your browser for unlimited usage.

2

Upload Job Description

Upload a JD file (PDF/DOCX) or paste the requirements text directly into the landing configurator.

3

Configure and Speak

Choose your mode (Technical, HR, etc.) and difficulty. Click Start Practice. Speak your answers using the microphone button or type them directly in the text editor. Use the Back button to navigate and edit previous questions.

4

Batch Evaluation

Submit and proceed directly to the next question. When done, click Finish Practice. All answers will evaluate at once in parallel, opening your detailed report and PDF download.

Gemini API Configuration

Provide your personal Gemini API Key if the backend is not pre-configured.

Your key is saved locally in your browser (localStorage) and sent directly to your Python backend to authenticate requests.
